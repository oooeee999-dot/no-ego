<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">每个人都有这种经历——</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">一句话，听过很多遍，没有感觉。
突然有一天，同样的话再想起来，整个人愣住了。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">你也一定读过，一本书，读过多次，回头忘记了。
但是在某个午后，晚上，或者早上，你突然再读，你突然有感而发。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">我说我自己，就我书面一本书。坛经，我特别，或者说我印象深刻：</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">五祖对六祖说“善护此念”。我那一刻深刻理解了那种情境。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(71, 193, 168); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">区别不在话上，在经历上。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">第一次听到的时候，生活还没有在身上刻过那道痕。那句话飘过来，没有地方可以落，像水滴在玻璃上，滑走了。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">后来被现实劈了一刀。那道伤口就是一个缺口。同样的话再飘过来，刚好嵌进去了。严丝合缝。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">这就是为什么同一本书，二十岁读和三十岁读完全不一样。书没变，字没变。多出来的是十年。十年在人身上刻了很多道痕，有些痕迹的形状，刚好跟书里某句话对得上。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">二十岁的时候，那句话从眼前飘过去了。三十岁的时候，一下子扎进来了。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">扎进来的那一刻叫"顿悟"。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(71, 193, 168); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">其实不是顿悟。是人终于长到了配得上那句话的地方。</strong></p>
<blockquote class="custom-blockquote multiquote-1" data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; padding-left: 20px; padding-right: 10px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; box-shadow: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0) 0px 0px 0px 0px; display: block; overflow-x: auto; overflow-y: auto;"><span style="display: block; color: rgb(85, 85, 85); font-size: 28px; line-height: 1.5em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; font-weight: bold;">“</span>

<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">不是不尊重说话的人。是真的听不懂。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">不是智商的问题。<strong style="color: rgb(71, 193, 168); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">是身体里还没有那个接收器。</strong> 那个接收器不是天生的，是被生活打出来的。每挨一次打，装一个零件。零件凑齐了，接收器才能工作。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(71, 193, 168); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">在那之前，所有的道理都是噪音。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">智慧跟时机的关系，比跟内容的关系大得多。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">同样一句"吃亏是福"——顺风顺水的时候听到，是废话。刚被人狠狠坑了一把、三年后却发现那次吃亏反而避开了一个更大的坑——这时候再想起这四个字，浑身一震。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">话没变。人变了。更准确地说——<strong style="color: rgb(71, 193, 168); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">身上终于长出了能接住这句话的那块肉。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">真正改变一个人的，从来不是道理。是事。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">是那些砸下来的、躲不开的、必须自己扛的事。</p>

<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(89, 89, 89); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">那道理还要不要讲？</p>
