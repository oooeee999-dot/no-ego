<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">He explained calmly. His daughter-in-law had been in a car accident at some point, and after that, her whole outlook changed — life's short, might as well spend it. She didn't work. His son's entire salary? Gone. Every last dollar, spent by the two of them.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">然后他补了一句，"在美国，怎么能不懂拿钱投资，光消费是会变负债的，还不是穷而已。"</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Then he added something that stuck with me: "In America, you can't afford not to invest. If all you do is spend, you don't just end up poor — you end up in debt."</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">我这个朋友，如果从读书来说，在咱国内都算精英。
结果去美国，依旧很会规划和布局投资。身价不菲，一个身价不菲的老大哥和我吐槽自己的儿子，那个表情，有种看开又看不开的感觉。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">My friend himself, by any measure, is an elite — highly educated, even by Chinese standards. In America, he stayed sharp, knew exactly how to plan and position his investments. Built serious wealth. But watching a man of that caliber complain about his own son — the look on his face was something else. Half resigned, half exasperated. Like he'd accepted it but couldn't quite let it go.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">这个事情好几年了。我依旧记得他说在美国要懂投资。可能在世界各个地方都需要懂，当可能美国对个人懂金融，懂投资，会更急迫一些。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">That conversation was years ago, but his words never left me: in America, you have to know how to invest. Maybe that's true everywhere in the world — but in America, the stakes are just higher. The urgency is real.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">我自己得出一个结论，不知道对不对：</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">I eventually came to my own conclusion. Not sure if it's right, but here it is:</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">一个国家越是"消费驱动型"的经济，老百姓就越需要懂金融。不懂金融，在这种国家里，你就是待宰的羔羊。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">The more consumer-driven a country's economy is, the more its people need to be financially literate. Without that knowledge, you're nothing but a lamb led to slaughter.</strong></p>

The Underlying Logic of a Consumer Nation</span><span class="suffix" style="display: none;"></span></h2>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">先说一个数字：美国经济里，消费占GDP的70%。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Let's start with one number: consumer spending makes up 70% of US GDP.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">这意味着什么？意味着美国这个国家的经济引擎，不是靠造东西，而是靠花钱。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Think about what that means. The American economic engine doesn't run on making things — it runs on people spending money.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">经济要转起来，就必须逼所有人把钱花出去。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">钱不能停。钱一停，经济就熄火。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">To keep that engine running, everyone has to be pushed into spending. Money has to keep moving. The second it stops, the whole thing stalls out.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">那怎么逼人花钱呢？</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">So how do you make people spend?</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">把存钱变成一件亏本的事。</strong></p>

<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">在这种设计下，你不动，就是在亏。</strong></p>

<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">And here's the truly absurd part: if you've never borrowed a dime and have no credit history, banks won't even lend to you. In America, owing nothing is the anomaly. Let that sink in — you can't borrow money unless you already owe money. It's almost a joke. Once you understand that, it makes perfect sense why America can owe the entire world and still act like everything's fine.</p>

<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">大概意思就是金融行为的弱肉强食。优胜劣汰。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">The idea is simple: survival of the fittest, but applied to money. <p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">在美国这种消费型国家里，人被金融知识分成了两个物种。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">In a consumer nation like America, financial knowledge splits people into two entirely different species.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">懂金融的人：用退休账户和基金跑赢通胀，用低息贷款撬动资产升值，用税法漏洞合法省钱，用正确的保险转移风险。结果？财富越滚越多，越来越富。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Those who get it: they park money in retirement accounts and index funds to outpace inflation, use cheap debt to leverage asset growth, exploit tax loopholes to keep more of what they earn, and pick the right insurance to offload risk. <p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">不懂金融的人：钱存银行被通胀吃掉，信用卡债越滚越大，不懂避税每年多交一大笔，不懂保险一场病回到解放前。结果？越来越穷，几乎不可逆。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Those who don't: their savings rot in a bank account while inflation chips away at them, credit card debt spirals out of control, they overpay on taxes year after year, and one medical emergency without proper coverage sends them right back to zero. <p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Why is the divide so extreme?</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">因为金融知识本身就是一道阶层壁垒。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Because financial literacy is itself a class barrier.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">富人家的孩子从小耳濡目染，有钱上好学校、请财务顾问、有本钱试错。穷人家的孩子没人教、没钱学、没机会试。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Rich kids grow up absorbing it by osmosis — good schools, financial advisors, and enough cushion to make mistakes and learn. Poor kids? Nobody teaches them, they can't afford to learn, and they never get the chance to experiment.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">懂金融的人越来越富，有更多资源去学更多金融知识，赚更多钱。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">The financially literate get richer, which gives them more resources to learn even more, which makes them even richer.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">不懂金融的人越来越穷，连学金融的机会都没有，只能继续被系统吃掉。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">The financially illiterate get poorer, until they can't even afford the chance to learn — and the system just keeps feeding on them.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">在消费型国家里，这是一个死循环。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">In a consumer-driven country, this is a death spiral.</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">你要么懂金融往上爬，要么不懂金融往下掉。没有中间地带。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">You either understand finance and climb, or you don't and you fall. There's no middle ground.</strong></p>

Why Production-Driven Nations Are a Different Story</span><span class="suffix" style="display: none;"></span></h2>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">说完美国，回头看看中国，你会发现一件有意思的事。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Now that we've covered America, let's turn to China. You'll notice something interesting.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">中国的经济结构跟美国完全不同。制造业占GDP的27%，全世界最高。消费占55%，投资占43%。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">China's economic structure is nothing like America's. Manufacturing accounts for 27% of GDP — the highest in the world. Consumption is 55%, investment 43%.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">经济增长不是只靠花钱，更靠造东西、修路架桥。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Growth isn't just powered by spending — it's driven by making things, building roads, and laying infrastructure.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">这种经济结构下，制度设计对普通人要"温和"得多。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Under this kind of structure, the system is far kinder to ordinary people.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">存款还有点收益。虽然跑不赢通胀，但好歹不是负利率，心里踏实有底。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Savings still earn something. It won't beat inflation, sure, but at least you're not dealing with negative interest rates. There's a floor under your feet.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">不需要靠债务活。中国人爱存钱不爱借钱，买房能多付首付就多付，很多人不怎么用信用卡。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">You don't need debt to get by. Chinese people are wired to save, not borrow. When buying a home, they put down as much as they can. Many barely touch credit cards.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">有基本医保兜底。虽然不完美，但不会因为一场阑尾炎手术欠五万美元。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">There's basic public health insurance as a safety net. Not perfect, but you're not going to owe $50,000 for an appendectomy.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">税收简单。工资税公司直接扣，大多数人一辈子不需要自己报税。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Taxes are straightforward. Your employer handles income tax. Most people never file a return in their entire life.</p>

A Deeper Question</span><span class="suffix" style="display: none;"></span></h2>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">一个更深的问题。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Let me raise a deeper question.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">一个国家让老百姓需要多"懂金融"才能活下去，某种程度上说明了这个国家的制度在服务谁。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">How much financial knowledge a country forces its citizens to have just to survive tells you a lot about who that system was built to serve.</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">如果不懂金融就会被系统吞掉，那这个系统的最大受益者是谁？</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">If financial ignorance means being swallowed whole, then who's really benefiting from that arrangement?</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">美国金融业占GDP的20%以上，中国只有7%-8%。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">The US financial sector accounts for over 20% of GDP. China's? Just 7%–8%.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">为什么美国金融业这么发达？因为制度把每个人都变成了金融消费者。你买保险，保险公司赚钱。你投资，基金公司收管理费。你贷款，银行赚利息。你避税，会计师赚咨询费。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Why is America's financial industry so enormous? Because the system turns every single person into a financial consumer. Buy insurance — the insurer profits. Invest — the fund charges fees. Borrow — the bank collects interest. Do tax planning — the accountant bills you. Everyone's a customer.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">每个美国人从出生到死亡，都在给金融行业交"过路费"。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">From the day they're born to the day they die, every American is paying tolls to the financial industry.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">华尔街那帮人为什么那么有钱？因为他们设计了一台精密的"金融抽水机"，从每个人身上抽。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Why is Wall Street so obscenely wealthy? Because they've engineered a finely tuned financial pump that siphons from everyone.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">消费型国家越发达，金融行业越赚钱，普通人就越需要懂金融才能不被抽干。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">The more advanced a consumer nation gets, the more money its financial industry makes — and the more ordinary people need to understand finance just to keep from being bled dry.</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">这是一个闭环。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">It's a closed loop.</p>
<hr data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 1px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: linear-gradient(90deg, rgba(60, 112, 198, 0) 0%, rgba(60, 122, 198, 0.75) 50%, rgba(60, 122, 198, 0) 100%); background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: 1px;">
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">所以："消费型国家是不是更需要懂金融？"</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">So: "Do people in consumer-driven nations need financial literacy more?"</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">不是"更需要"，是"不懂就活不下去"。</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-weight: bold; background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: top; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; width: auto; height: auto; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border-top-style: none; border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-right-style: none; border-top-width: 3px; border-bottom-width: 3px; border-left-width: 3px; border-right-width: 3px; border-top-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-bottom-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-left-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-right-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4); border-top-left-radius: 0px; border-top-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-right-radius: 0px; border-bottom-left-radius: 0px;">It's not that they "need it more." It's that they can't survive without it.</strong></p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">在消费型国家，金融知识就是生存技能。懂的人如鱼得水，不懂的人慢慢被吞噬。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">In a consumer nation, financial literacy is a survival skill. Those who have it thrive. Those who don't get consumed — slowly, quietly, and completely.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">在生产型国家，金融知识是加分项，不是必选项。你不懂，也能靠勤劳过日子。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">In a production nation, financial literacy is a nice-to-have, not a must-have. You can still get by on good old-fashioned hard work.</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">当然，这话我也不敢说得太绝对。中国的房价、医疗、养老，也让很多人焦虑。哪个国家都有自己的问题。</p>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="color: rgb(74, 74, 74); font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.8em; letter-spacing: 0.02em; text-align: left; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 8px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px;">Of course, I wouldn't be too absolute about this. China's housing prices, healthcare costs, and pension pressures give plenty of people anxiety too. No country is without its own set of problems.</p>
